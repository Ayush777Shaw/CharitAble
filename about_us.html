About Our Charity

In this project we are providing a facility for the needy ones where anyone can donate clothes, money, books or leftover food from any event to them. Both the parties will be connected via a medium who will work for the organisation profitably. We are trying to ensure that no-one sleeps without an empty stomach or without a cloth on. We have a team of 5 passionate members who are determined to make this project a success and with perfection.

CharitAble is an NGO in India based on kolkata. Established in 2023, we have worked extensively for several months now to reach out and rehabilitate differently abled people from underprivileged segment.
